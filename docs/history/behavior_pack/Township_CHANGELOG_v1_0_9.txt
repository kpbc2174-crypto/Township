Township CHANGELOG v1.0.9

Stable base: v1.0.7/v1.0.8 lifecycle behavior.

Changes in v1.0.9:
- Improved starter camp layout.
- Replaced the janky internal log/wall layout with a cleaner open pavilion.
- Starter camp now uses a 9x9 prepared area, four corner posts, an open shelter, and a simple roof.
- Added faster skip logic for already-correct blocks.
- Air entries that are already air are now skipped immediately instead of consuming one build delay each.
- Kept Android crouch-near-Founding-Stone status behavior.
- Kept builder nudge-back behavior during construction.

Known issues:
- The Township Builder is still a vanilla villager and can wander outside active construction moments.
- The starter camp is still a prototype structure, not the final central lot/town hall design.
