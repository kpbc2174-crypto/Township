Township v1.0.40

Update method:
- Same fixed Township BP/RP/module UUID identity retained.
- Version bumped to 1.0.40 only.
- No claim radius change.
- No build radius change.
- No lot marker placement rule change.
- No ground-level rule change.
- No boundary radius change.

Added automation:
- Added Auto Place Lots toggle to the Township Founding Stone UI.
- Auto Place Lots is OFF by default.
- When turned ON, the township automatically places new lot markers and queues their normal prep/build/road workflow.
- Auto placement tests all three lot sizes in the same automation pass: large, medium, and small.
- Test limits are larger:
  - Small Lots: 12
  - Medium Lots: 8
  - Large Lots: 5
- Auto placement waits until township prep is complete and the starter/main lot is complete.
- Auto placement respects existing lot overlap rules and town-center spacing.
- Auto placement uses the existing lot-prep, auto-build, and auto-road systems.

UI changes:
- Founding Stone UI now shows Auto Place Lots status.
- Founding Stone status now shows auto lot limits.

Testing notes:
1. Place a fresh Township Founding Stone.
2. Wait for quadrant prep, boundary, and starter/main lot completion.
3. Open the Founding Stone UI.
4. Turn Auto Place Lots ON.
5. Confirm it places small, medium, and large lot markers automatically.
6. Confirm lots do not overlap and then proceed through prep/build/road normally.
