Township v1.0.21 Changelog

Stable base: v1.0.20.

Changes:
- Added Township Ground Leveler block.
- Ground Leveler queues a large 31x31 leveling job inside an active township build radius.
- Ground Leveler clears 12 blocks above its placement level and levels the ground to its base/floor level.
- Ground Leveler preserves the Ground Leveler block itself and township control blocks.
- Roads are now wider: prototype road jobs place a 3-wide/brush-style Township Dirt Road path.
- Changed lot job ordering so a completed lot prep prioritizes that lot's house before moving to the next lot.
- Changed house completion to prioritize that lot's road before moving to the next queued lot.
- Kept automatic full-lot clearing for now.
- Kept directional Founding Stone, directional Lot Marker, Android crouch status, and fresh UUID import behavior.

Known limits:
- Ground Leveler is a blunt testing utility and can remove player-built terrain/features in its 31x31 area.
- Road routing is still prototype Manhattan routing and can cut through terrain.
- Door placement still needs multi-direction testing on device.
