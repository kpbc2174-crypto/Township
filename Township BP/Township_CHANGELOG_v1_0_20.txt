Township v1.0.20

Status:
- Built from the working v1.0.19 base.
- Fresh UUIDs for clean Android import beside older test builds.

Changes:
- Kept the real oak door and real red bed from v1.0.19.
- Added Township Dirt Road as the first custom road block.
- Added Township Dirt Road to the Township creative group/search.
- After a Small House completes, the builder now queues and builds a simple dirt road from the lot front toward the starter camp front opening.
- Lot status now includes Road Phase as well as Road Status.
- Added a small porch to the Small House front.
- Added more stable full-block exterior detail, including a simple chimney.
- Added a furnace inside the Small House.
- Increased house and road work batch sizes slightly for faster testing.

Known limitations:
- Road routing is a simple Manhattan path and may cut straight through rough terrain.
- Road connection is still prototype logic, not the final auto-road network.
- Door/bed orientation should be tested from several Lot Marker facing directions.
