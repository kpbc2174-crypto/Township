Township CHANGELOG v1.0.36

Update method:
- Same fixed BP/RP/module UUIDs retained.
- Version bumped to 1.0.36 only.

Fixes:
- Corrected lot/building ground-level math globally.
- Township Lot Markers remain one block above prepared dirt.
- Building floors, paths, and porches now build on the prepared dirt surface at markerY - 1.
- Walls, doors, beds, furniture, fences, and workstation blocks now sit at markerY.
- This should stop buildings from appearing raised or from burying lot markers.
- Founding Stone town-prep rule from v1.0.35 is retained: dirt fills to one block below the Founding Stone.

No intentional changes:
- No claim radius/build radius change.
- No lot marker placement rule change.
- No township boundary radius change.
- No building selection change.

Test:
1. Place a fresh Township Founding Stone.
2. Confirm the Founding Stone stays one block above the dirt surface.
3. Place small, medium, and large Lot Markers.
4. Confirm each marker remains one block above prepared dirt.
5. Confirm buildings sit on the prepared surface instead of floating/raised or digging dips.
