Township v1.0.34 Changelog

Update workflow
- Fixed UUID update workflow retained.
- BP/RP/module UUIDs unchanged from the stable Township identity.
- Manifest/module/dependency versions bumped to 1.0.34 only.

Lot and building ground fixes
- Lot prep no longer clears the lot surface layer before leveling.
- Lot prep now fills normal dirt up to the marker placement level, not one block too low.
- Building plans now use the corrected lot surface level so houses should sit on the prepared lot instead of above a dip.
- Lot boundary fences are placed on top of the new surface layer instead of replacing the ground.
- House floor repair now restores the entire lot surface before the building is placed.

Township founding area
- Visible township prep boundary radius re-expanded from 32 to 64 for this test build.
- Founding area prep radius re-expanded to 64.
- Clear height remains limited at 16 blocks above and 2 blocks below the founding level to avoid the previous oversized clearing queue.
- Normal dirt remains the top fill block so grass can regrow.

Ground Leveler
- Township Ground Leveler now removes/breaks itself when its job completes.

Farm/building cleanup
- Farm buildings now place the farm patch outside/in the yard area instead of as a cramped strip inside the house or under furniture.
- Farm patches include farmland, wheat, water, and a composter as the temporary farmer job block.
- Beds were moved deeper against rear/side walls so they should no longer block the doorway.
- Build speed increased for lot and building work to help catch incomplete structures during testing.

Still tracking
- Preset buildings still need a proper design pass.
- Township Build Recorder remains included but was not the focus of this build.
- Outer boundary at larger radius may still depend on loaded/simulated chunks; test from near the Founding Stone first.
