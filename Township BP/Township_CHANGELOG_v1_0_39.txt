Township v1.0.39

Update method:
- Same fixed BP/RP/module UUID identity as the previous fixed-UUID builds.
- Version bumped to 1.0.39 only.
- No claim radius, build radius, lot marker placement, ground-level, or boundary radius change in this build.

Added Founding Stone UI:
- Tap/use or crouch near the Township Founding Stone to open an Android-friendly menu.
- The menu shows township status and basic automation controls.

Added automation settings:
- Auto Build Lots toggle.
  - ON: prepared empty lots automatically queue a random building.
  - OFF: lots prepare and stay ready/empty.
- Auto Roads toggle.
  - ON: completed buildings queue road connections.
  - OFF: completed buildings do not automatically queue roads.
- Pause/Resume Builder toggle.
  - Pauses normal builder work without changing township records.
  - Town prep still remains separate.

Added manual recovery/actions:
- Rebuild Outer Boundary.
  - Queues a boundary-only rebuild without clearing or leveling terrain.
- Clear Stuck Queue.
  - Clears non-town-prep queued jobs as a recovery tool.
- Show Status in Chat.

Kept:
- v1.0.38 quadrant township setup.
- v1.0.36 ground-level rules.
- Township Build Recorder remains included.
